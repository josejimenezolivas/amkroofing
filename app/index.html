The pipeline

From shingle to sequence

We turn idle roof area into a vertically integrated energy-to-compute stack — generation and processing living in the same square footage.

STEP 01

Solar roofing

We re-skin the roof with high-density solar roofing — weatherproof, building-integrated, engineered for decades.

STEP 02

Clean generation

Sunlight becomes DC power on-site — no transmission losses, no grid queue, no fossil baseline.

STEP 03

Edge GPU cluster

Power feeds a containerized GPU node beneath the roofline — one tile in a distributed, solar-native compute fabric.

STEP 04

Biotech workloads

Protein folding, genomic pipelines, and molecular simulation run on the clean compute — discovery, powered by daylight.

Why distributed + solar

Compute where the sun already falls.

Hyperscale data centers strain grids and chase water for cooling. We invert the model: thousands of small, solar-skinned roofs become a distributed cluster — generating power exactly where it's consumed.

Zero transmission loss

Generation and compute sit centimeters apart — power never touches the grid.

Grid-friendly by design

No interconnection queue, no new substations — capacity scales one roof at a time.

Passive thermal advantage

Open rooftop airflow cuts the cooling load that forces inland data centers to burn water and watts.
